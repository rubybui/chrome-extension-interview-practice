Interview Questions

1 / 5

Tell me about yourself

Walk me through your background, experience, and what brings you to this role.

Tips:

  • Keep it under 2 minutes
  • Focus on relevant experience
  • Connect to the role you're applying for
  • End with why you're interested

What are your greatest strengths?

Describe your key strengths and how they would benefit this position.

Tips:

  • Choose 2-3 relevant strengths
  • Provide specific examples
  • Connect to job requirements
  • Show how you've used them

What is your greatest weakness?

Discuss a weakness you've identified and how you're working to improve it.

Tips:

  • Choose a genuine weakness
  • Show self-awareness
  • Demonstrate improvement efforts
  • Keep it professional

Why do you want this job?

Explain your motivation for applying and what excites you about this opportunity.

Tips:

  • Research the company thoroughly
  • Connect to your career goals
  • Mention specific aspects of the role
  • Show enthusiasm and fit

Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Share your career aspirations and how this role fits into your long-term plans.

Tips:

  • Show ambition but be realistic
  • Connect to the company's growth
  • Demonstrate commitment to growth
  • Align with the role's progression
